# Nature 写作风格指南

## 核心原则

### 1. 简洁性 (Conciseness)

**目标**：用最少的词传达最多的信息

**规则**：
- 每句 ≤30 词
- 避免冗余表达
- 删除不必要的修饰词
- 使用主动语态

**示例**：
```
❌ It is important to note that the results of our experiment clearly demonstrate...
✅ Our results show...
```

### 2. 清晰性 (Clarity)

**目标**：让非专业读者也能理解核心信息

**规则**：
- 避免过度技术化术语
- 使用具体而非抽象的表达
- 逻辑结构清晰
- 段落主题明确

**示例**：
```
❌ The utilization of advanced computational methodologies facilitates...
✅ We use machine learning to...
```

### 3. 故事弧 (Story Arc)

**目标**：构建引人入胜的科学叙事

**结构**：
1. **Context**: 广泛背景和重要性
2. **Gap**: 现有知识的空白
3. **Discovery**: 本文的发现
4. **Implication**: 发现的意义和影响

**示例**：
```
Context: Climate change threatens global food security.
Gap: However, the molecular mechanisms of heat tolerance remain poorly understood.
Discovery: Here we identify a key gene that confers heat tolerance in rice.
Implication: This finding enables development of heat-resistant crops.
```

## 语言规范

### 句子结构

**长度**：
- 平均句长：15-20 词
- 最大句长：30 词
- 句长变化：混合短句和长句

**结构**：
- 避免嵌套从句
- 一个句子一个观点
- 使用连接词保持流畅

### 段落结构

**长度**：
- 平均段落：100-200 词
- 最大段落：300 词

**结构**：
- 主题句：明确表达段落核心观点
- 支撑句：提供证据和细节
- 过渡句：连接到下一段

### 词汇选择

**优先使用**：
- 具体动词：show, demonstrate, reveal, identify
- 简单词汇：use 而非 utilize, help 而非 facilitate
- 主动语态：We found 而非 It was found

**避免使用**：
- AI 特征词汇：tapestry, delve, showcase, pivotal
- 过度修饰：groundbreaking, revolutionary, novel (过多)
- 模糊表达：very, quite, rather, somewhat

## 时态规范

### Abstract

- **过去时**：描述本文发现 (We found, Our results showed)
- **现在时**：陈述普遍真理 (Climate change is...)

### Introduction

- **现在时**：背景知识 (Climate change threatens...)
- **现在完成时**：近期研究 (Previous studies have shown...)
- **过去时**：具体研究 (Smith et al. found...)

### Methods

- **过去时**：描述实验 (We collected samples, We performed PCR)

### Results

- **过去时**：描述结果 (We observed, The data showed)

### Discussion

- **现在时**：解释意义 (Our findings suggest...)
- **过去时**：引用本文结果 (We found...)
- **对冲语言**：可能、推测 (may, could, suggests)

---

## 对冲校准 (Hedging Calibration)

### 证据强度匹配

| 证据水平 | 对冲表达 |
|----------|----------|
| 强证据 | show, demonstrate, establish |
| 中等证据 | suggest, indicate, support |
| 弱证据 | may, could, might |
| 推测 | speculate, hypothesize, propose |

### 示例

```
强证据: Our data show that X causes Y.
中等证据: Our results suggest that X may contribute to Y.
弱证据: These findings indicate a possible link between X and Y.
推测: We speculate that X might play a role in Y.
```

### 避免的表达

- 过度自信：prove, confirm, establish (除非证据确凿)
- 过度对冲：might possibly, could potentially

## 段落写作

### 段落类型

**1. 主题段 (Topic Paragraph)**
- 位置：章节开头
- 功能：提出核心观点
- 结构：观点 → 解释 → 预告

**2. 证据段 (Evidence Paragraph)**
- 位置：章节中间
- 功能：提供支持证据
- 结构：主张 → 证据 → 分析

**3. 过渡段 (Transition Paragraph)**
- 位置：章节之间
- 功能：连接不同部分
- 结构：总结前文 → 引出下文

### 段落连接

**逻辑连接词**：
- 因果：therefore, thus, consequently
- 对比：however, whereas, although
- 补充：moreover, furthermore, additionally
- 举例：for example, specifically, in particular

**段落过渡**：
- 重复关键词
- 使用代词指代
- 提出问题并回答

## Nature 风格示例

### 好的写作

```
Climate change threatens global food security. Rising temperatures reduce crop yields 
by disrupting photosynthesis and accelerating senescence. However, the molecular 
mechanisms underlying heat tolerance remain poorly understood. Here we identify 
HEAT-TOLERANT 1 (HT1), a transcription factor that activates heat-shock genes in rice. 
Plants overexpressing HT1 show 30% higher yields under heat stress. Our findings 
reveal a key genetic target for breeding heat-resistant crops.
```

**优点**：
- 每句 ≤25 词
- 清晰的故事弧
- 具体的数据 (30%)
- 明确的意义

### 需要改进的写作

```
In recent years, climate change has become an increasingly important topic in 
agricultural research. Many studies have investigated the effects of temperature on 
crop production, but the molecular mechanisms are still not fully understood. In this 
paper, we present a novel transcription factor that we have identified through our 
research. Our experimental results demonstrate that this gene significantly improves 
heat tolerance in rice plants.
```

**问题**：
- 句子过长
- 模糊表达 (many, significantly)
- 缺乏具体数据
- 故事弧不清晰
