# 伦理审查清单

## 核心原则

### 1. 尊重自主性

**要求**：
- 知情同意
- 自愿参与
- 隐私保护
- 数据保密

### 2. 善行

**要求**：
- 最大化利益
- 最小化风险
- 风险收益比合理
- 社会价值

### 3. 公正

**要求**：
- 公平选择受试者
- 公平分配负担和利益
- 弱势群体保护
- 资源公平分配

### 4. 诚信

**要求**：
- 研究诚信
- 数据真实
- 报告完整
- 利益冲突声明

## 动物研究伦理

### 1. 3R 原则

**Replacement (替代)**：
- 使用替代方法
- 减少动物使用
- 优化实验设计

- 最小样本量
- 优化实验设计
- 提高数据质量

**Refinement (优化)**：
- 减轻疼痛
- 改善福利

## 伦理声明模板

### 1. 伦理批准声明

**模板**：
```
This study was approved by the Institutional Review Board of [Institution] 
(approval number: [Number], date: [Date]). All participants provided written 
informed consent before participation.
```

### 2. 知情同意声明

**模板**：
```
All participants provided written informed consent after being informed of 
the study purpose, procedures, risks, and benefits. Participation was voluntary 
and participants could withdraw at any time without penalty.
```

### 3. 动物伦理声明

**模板**：
```
All animal procedures were approved by the Institutional Animal Care and Use 
Committee of [Institution] (approval number: [Number], date: [Date]). All 
experiments were performed in accordance with relevant guidelines and regulations.
```

### 4. 数据伦理声明

**模板**：
```
All data were collected, stored, and analyzed in accordance with applicable 
data protection regulations. Personal data were anonymized before analysis 
and will be securely stored for [time period] after study completion.
```

### 5. 利益冲突声明

**模板**：
```
The authors declare no competing interests. [If conflicts exist: Author A 
has received consulting fees from Company X. Author B holds stock in Company Y. 
These interests did not influence the study design, data analysis, or 
interpretation of results.]
```

### 6. AI 使用声明

**模板**：
```
[AI tool name] was used during this study for [specific purpose, e.g., 
language editing, data analysis]. All AI-generated content was reviewed 
and verified by the authors. The authors are responsible for the accuracy 
and integrity of all reported findings.
```
